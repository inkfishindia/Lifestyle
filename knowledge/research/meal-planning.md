# Meal Planning Research Reference

## Chrono-Nutrition for Night Owls

| Rule | Implementation |
|------|---------------|
| Eat within 1.5 hrs of waking | Breakfast by 11:30 AM latest |
| Largest meal at biological midday | Lunch at 12:30 PM — peak insulin sensitivity ~6 hrs post-wake |
| Pre-workout fuel | Snack at 4:30-5 PM, every sport day |
| Dinner before biological evening | By 9 PM (fine for night-owl chronotype) |
| Stop eating 2.5 hrs before sleep | If sleeping 2:30 AM, last food by midnight |
| No caffeine in biological evening | Cut caffeine by 8:30-9 PM |

## Why Skipping Lunch Crashes Everything
- Blood glucose drops → prefrontal cortex underperforms
- Cortisol spikes → impairs focus, raises anxiety
- Peripheral clocks desynchronize → compounds over days
- This is hormonal, not just caloric. The 12:30 lunch is infrastructure.

## Late-Night Creative Fuel
| Food | Why | Avoid |
|------|-----|-------|
| Makhana (fox nuts) | Slow-release, no crash | Chips/biscuits (crash in 45 min) |
| Dark chocolate 70%+ | Magnesium, dopamine | Milk chocolate (sugar crash) |
| Walnuts | Omega-3, brain fuel | Heavy fried foods |
| Warm turmeric milk | Tryptophan → melatonin for sleep | Coffee after 8:30 PM |

## Hydration Target: 3-3.5L/day
Tender coconut water, buttermilk, electrolytes around sport. Bangalore climate dehydrates.
