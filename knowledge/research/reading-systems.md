# Reading & Learning Systems Research Reference

## Naval Ravikant's Philosophy

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| "Read what you love until you love to read" | Genre doesn't matter. Fiction counts. Follow curiosity. |
| Drop books freely | No sunk cost. Chapter 1 doesn't grab you → move on. |
| Read 10-20 simultaneously | Keep Kindle loaded. Flip based on mood. Mix genres. |
| Re-read > new books | Great book read twice > two average books once |
| No fixed schedule | Read when pulled, not scheduled. Create environment that pulls. |

## Ali Abdaal's Learning System

| Technique | What You DO | Time |
|-----------|------------|------|
| Active Recall | Close book, write what you remember. Testing > re-reading. | After each session |
| 3-Highlight Method | Capture exactly 3 key ideas per book/pod. No more. | 1 min |
| Spaced Repetition | Use Readwise ($8/mo) to auto-resurface highlights daily | 2 min/day |
| Feel-Good framing | Make learning feel like play. Connect to what you're building. | Mindset |

## Reading Habit Formation

| Factor | Evidence | Application |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Format doesn't matter | No significant difference in comprehension: reading vs audiobooks | Drive time = valid reading. Audiobooks count. |
| Environment design | 3x more likely to maintain habit with designed environment | Kindle on pillow. Phone across room. |
| Start absurdly small | 5 minutes is research-backed starting point | Set timer for 5 min. Most nights you'll go to 15-20. |
| Identity shift | "The goal is to become a reader" not "read a book" | Every 5-min session = a vote for "I'm someone who reads" |

## Retention for Application

| Technique | How | Time |
|-----------|-----|------|
| Feynman Technique | Explain in simple language. Where you stumble = don't understand. | 3 min voice-note |
| Implementation Intentions | "If [situation], then [behavior]." One insight → one rule. | 1 min |
| 24-Hour Rule | Try to recall 3 highlights next morning before checking notes | 2 min |
| Voice-note capture | After podcast drive, record 3 takeaways before exiting car | 30 sec |
